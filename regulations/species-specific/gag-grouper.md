# Gag Grouper (*Mycteroperca microlepis*)

## Current Regulations (as of Amendment 30)

### Recreational Fishery
- **Minimum Size Limit**: 20 inches total length
- **Bag Limit**: 2 fish per person per day

### Commercial Fishery
- **Minimum Size Limit**: 20 inches total length
- **Trip Limit**: 1,000 pounds gutted weight

## Regulatory Rationale
The current size and bag limits are designed to maintain sustainable harvest levels while allowing for recreational and commercial fishing opportunities. These limits were established based on stock assessment data from 2015 and are subject to change as new data becomes available.
